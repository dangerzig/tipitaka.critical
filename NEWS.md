# tipitaka.critical 0.1.0

* Initial release with lemmatized critical edition of the complete Pali Canon.
* 5,777 text units across all three pitakas (Sutta, Vinaya, Abhidhamma).
* 2.8 million tokens with 98% unique-word coverage via Digital Pali Dictionary.
* Five-witness collation: PTS/GRETIL, SuttaCentral, VRI, BJT, Thai.
* Ships only text data; frequency tables and sparse matrix computed on first use.
